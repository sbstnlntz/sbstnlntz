<h2>NAME</h2>
<p>
&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;sebastian.service - lifecycle and runtime behavior description
</p>

<h2>LIFECYCLE</h2>
<p>
&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;Startup begins with caffeine.<br>
&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;Active during coding, problem-solving and creation.<br>
&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;Maintenance performed through breaks and hydration.<br>
&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;Graceful shutdown is recommended.
</p>

<h2>RESTART POLICY</h2>
<p>
&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;OnFailure – restart with debugging and analysis.<br>
&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;OnImprovement – restart to apply new insights or ideas.
</p>
