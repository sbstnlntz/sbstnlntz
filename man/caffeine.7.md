<h2>NAME</h2>
<p>&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;caffeine - unofficial runtime dependency</p>

<h2>DESCRIPTION</h2>
<p>
&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;Provides increased focus, creativity and mental performance.<br>
&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;Optional, but highly effective.
</p>

<h2>MODES</h2>

<pre>
--coffee    Strong, fast, default
--tea       Gentle, slow, stable
--none      Detox mode
</pre>

<h2>EFFECTS</h2>
<p>
&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;Boosts idea generation and concentration.<br>
&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;May cause jitter when overdosed.
</p>
