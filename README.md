<h2>NAME</h2>
<p>
&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;sebastian - full-stack developer with a caffeine-powered mindset
</p>

<h2>SYNOPSIS</h2>
<p>
&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;sebastian [--code] [--design] [--build] [--coffee]
</p>

<h2>DESCRIPTION</h2>
<p>
&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;I am Sebastian, a full-stack developer who enjoys writing clean,<br>
&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;maintainable and reliable software. I move comfortably between<br>
&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;backend logic, user interfaces and deployment workflows.
</p>

<p>
&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;“Engineering logic, crafting UI  – running on caffeine.”
</p>

<p>
&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;I like architecture that makes sense, code that reads well,<br>
&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;and systems that stay stable in the long run. I learn fast,<br>
&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;experiment often and build things that feel practical<br>
&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;and purposeful.
</p>

</p>

<h2>OPTIONS</h2>

<p>
&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;<b>--code</b><br>
&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;Activates my development mode. Backend or frontend,<br>
&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;whichever needs solving.
</p>

<p>
&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;<b>--design</b><br>
&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;Enables my UI/UX focus. Structure, flow and clarity.
</p>

<p>
&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;<b>--build</b><br>
&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;Produces production-ready deployments, pipelines<br>
&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;and stable environments.
</p>

<p>
&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;<b>--coffee</b><br>
&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;Boosts creativity, energy and execution speed.
</p>
